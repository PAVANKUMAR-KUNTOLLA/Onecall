Refund Status

To check the status of your Federal and State refunds, we have provided web links for your convenience.

You can access the refund status for the current year (Tax Year 2023) once your E-file tax return has been acknowledged. If you filed a paper tax return, please allow 4 weeks for the status to be available online.

The processing time for Federal and State refunds varies based on the filing method. Form 1040X (Amended Tax Return) refunds take 12 weeks to process. It's recommended to check the status only after 10 weeks have passed.
